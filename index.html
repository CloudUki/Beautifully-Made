About Beautifully Made

Beautifully Made is a wedding hair and makeup company based in
Akron, Pennsylvania. Their team of highly skilled makeup artists and
hairstylists prides themselves on enhancing the natural beauty of their
clients, empowering them to feel confident on their wedding day.
Drawing on their years of experience in the beauty industry, the team
is proud to provide you with warm, friendly, and professional service
on your wedding day.
